Podcast - Daydream Corner

"Daydream Corner" is produced by a 23-year-old Taiwanese woman, who shares her journey of studying abroad in California, USA, and explores the bittersweet experiences of personal growth. The podcast covers various themes including study abroad diaries, personal growth journals, and segments where books are discussed entirely in English. Whether you're interested in the life of a student studying abroad or curious about someone else's journey of personal development, this podcast offers inspiration and resonance.
